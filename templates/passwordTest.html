Understanding Password Security

How Password Cracking Works: Password cracking involves guessing passwords until the correct one is found. This can be done through various methods like brute-force attacks, dictionary attacks, and more.

Improving Your Password: Use a combination of letters, numbers, and symbols. Avoid common words and patterns. Longer passwords are generally more secure.
